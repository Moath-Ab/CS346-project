Welcome To the Futrue of The Desert.

About AL-ULA

Al-Ula is one of the cities of the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia. It is located in the northwest of the Kingdom. It is administratively affiliated to the Emirate of Al-Madinah Al-Munawwarah Region. It is approximately 300 km north of the center of the Emirate. It is the capital of Al-Ula Governorate. Human settlement is about 4,000 years old, and it is one of the most important tourist destinations in Saudi Arabia. Yaqut al-Hamawi mentioned al-Ula in his dictionary, with the inclusion of its first, and al-Qasr.

absorptive capacity This is accompanied by a variety of food choices and accommodation facilities. 5,000 rooms is the total capacity for eco-lodges, luxury hotels, resorts, and canyon plantations carved into the sandstone rock. By 2035, this capacity will be nearly double what it is now.

Activties

1- The first festival dedicated to the culinary arts We invite food lovers to the first festival dedicated to celebrating the culinary arts in AlUla, from December 29 to January 14, and enjoy new flavors and unique aromas from around the world. The festival includes many activities designed to introduce guests to the world of cooking and its secrets, with professional lessons supervised by a group of famous chefs.

2- A tour on horse-drawn carriages in Al-Hijr We invite you to explore Al-Hijr, the first UNESCO World Heritage Site in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia, with a guided tour on traditional horse carriages at sunrise or sunset. Contemplate the charm of civilization and the beauty of creativity in an enjoyable experience with which you live the life of the Nabataeans on wooden carts made with the utmost craftsmanship.

Resturents

1-Alpha Lounge Alpha Lounge is distinguished by its quiet location and relaxing atmosphere amid the natural landscapes in one of the desert valleys of Al-Ula. An ideal destination, we advise Al-Ula guests to visit to enjoy a cup of coffee among the most amazing desert terrain, or a delicious dinner under the stars. Daily from 6:00 am to 1:00 am.

2- Beit Wafa Bait Wafa Restaurant is distinguished by its location among the Al-Ula Mountains in the luxurious camping site “26 North”, with the most amazing landscapes as far as the eye can see. A varied menu carefully prepared in the home style from the finest and freshest ingredients produced by the neighboring local farms
